How I got here

I've always loved to build things since I was young. That trend continued through every phase of my life; starting from legos, to playing resource gathering board games, and then, professionally, with project management and Tableau. That last piece was my gateway into conputer science and then learning about software engineering and, from there, I just fell in love with the freedom of programming.
